Our Story

Founded in 2012 by Chef Amir Hassan, Olive & Thyme began as a tribute to his grandmother’s recipes from the Aegean coast.

We blend the vibrant flavors of Greece, Morocco, and Southern Italy with modern techniques and locally sourced produce.

Every dish is a celebration of sun, sea, and shared moments around the table.
